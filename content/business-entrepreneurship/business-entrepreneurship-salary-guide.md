## How to Read This Guide

Salaries vary by:
- **Experience level**: Entry (0-2 years), Mid (3-8 years), Senior (9+ years)
- **Company stage**: Startup (higher risk, equity upside), Mid-market, Enterprise (stable, predictable)
- **Location**: Metro (Bengaluru, Delhi, Mumbai) pay 20-40% more than tier-2 cities
- **Education**: MBA adds ₹10-15 lakh to base salary at top consulting firms

All figures are **annual base salary** (LPA = lakh per annum in India; US is annual in USD). Bonuses, stock options, and benefits can add 20-50%.

## Business Analyst

Your role: Analyze business processes, recommend improvements, solve operational problems.

| Level | India (₹ LPA) | US ($) | Typical Employer |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Entry (0-2 years)** | ₹3-7L | $65K-$90K | IT services, banks, startups |
| **Mid (3-7 years)** | ₹6-12L | $85K-$110K | Tech companies, consulting firms |
| **Senior (8+ years)** | ₹15-23L | $115K-$140K | Top tech, MNC consulting |

**Entry path**: Graduate → Business Analyst Trainee → Business Analyst → Senior Analyst.

**Fastest growth**: Top tech companies (Amazon, Google, Flipkart) and consulting firms offer quicker jumps.

## Management Consultant

Your role: Advise companies on strategy, operations, organizational change.

| Level | India (₹ LPA) | US ($) | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Entry (Pre-MBA, 0-3 yrs)** | ₹15-20L | $120K-$135K | Associate Consultant, Project Lead |
| **Entry (Post-MBA, 0-2 yrs)** | ₹30-40L | $192K-$210K | Business Analyst role but MBA fast-track |
| **Mid (5-8 yrs)** | ₹50-70L | $250K-$330K | Manager, Senior Manager |
| **Senior (10+ yrs)** | ₹80L-1.5Cr | $400K-$600K | Partner Track, Principal Consultant |

**Big Three** (McKinsey, BCG, Bain): Premium 20-30% above market.
**Big Four** (Deloitte, PwC, EY, KPMG): Tier-2 premium, strong growth.
**Boutique/Indian firms**: Lower base but good equity upside for founders.

**Bonus structure**: 20-40% of base at entry, 50-100%+ at partner levels.

## Negotiation Tips

**Entry-level** (0-2 years): Focus on learning, accept 10-15% below max in exchange for brand/skill.

**Mid-career** (3-7 years): You have leverage. Aim for 15-25% bump when switching companies.

**Senior-level** (8+ years): Negotiate equity/performance bonus heavily. Base salary matters less than upside.

**Red flags**: If offered below range + promised "equity later," be cautious. Equity should be on paper.

## Total Compensation Reality Check

**Base salary is 60-70% of real earnings.** Factor in:

- **Bonus**: 15-50% of base (consulting: 25-100%+)
- **Equity**: 0-200% of base (startups: high, corporates: low)
- **Benefits**: Health, retirement, perks (₹2-10L value)
- **Flexibility**: Remote work, flexible hours (worth ₹3-8L if valued)

A ₹10L base + 30% bonus + ₹4L equity + ₹5L benefits = ₹19.5L real comp.

## Your Personal Salary Growth Strategy

**Years 1-3**: Build expertise, accept lower pay for learning. Aim to move from ₹4L → ₹8L.

**Years 4-6**: You're valuable. Jump companies to unlock 30-50% jumps. ₹8L → ₹15L.

**Years 7-10**: Specialize (partner track, C-suite path). ₹15L → ₹40-80L.

**10+ years**: Leadership premium dominates. ₹80L-3Cr.

The gap between staying and switching: **Staying put costs you ₹20-50L in lost compound growth.**
